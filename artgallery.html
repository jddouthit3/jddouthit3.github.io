My Process

Believe it or not, I do not use Photoshop to create my artwork. I start by taking a photo of something--it could be anything from a photo of a door to an ugly selfie. Using various apps like Chroma Lab, Glitch Lab, and Mirror Lab, I absolute destroy the photo until it looks cool like the images below. Naturally, these images have a high degree of geometry and symmetry. Enjoy browsing.
