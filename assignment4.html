Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

There are a few accessibility concerns that these challenges might present to the viewer of the site. For challenge 2, the chess pieces may be a little small, so viewers might find it difficult to see them. In fact, across the site, the font might be hard to read for those with vision issues. Making things larger would go a long way. Another possible issue is that there may not be enough alt text available. People that rely on alt text may have trouble navigating through the website because of this. Overall, there are definitely some things that are concerning with regards to accessibility.
